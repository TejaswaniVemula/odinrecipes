Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with fragrant basmati rice, spices, and marinated meat or vegetables. It is a popular dish in South Asia.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or Mutton
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Marinate the Meat with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook the Rice with whole spices.
  3. Fry Onions and layer them over the rice.
  4. Layer Meat and Rice.
  5. Dum Cook for perfect flavor.
  6. Serve and Enjoy.
