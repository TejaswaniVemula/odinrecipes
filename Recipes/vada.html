Vada

Description

Vada is a popular South Indian snack made from ground lentils, spices, and herbs. It is deep-fried until crispy and is usually served with chutney or sambar.

Ingredients

  • Urad dal (Black gram lentils)
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Curry leaves
  • Oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Soak and grind Urad Dal to a smooth batter.
  2. Add spices and herbs.
  3. Shape the batter into doughnut-like rounds.
  4. Deep-fry until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
